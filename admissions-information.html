In order to conform to the new situation of economic globalization and internationalization of education, according to the spirit of Ganjiao Gaoxie [2002] No. 97 document, with the approval of Jiangxi Provincial Department of Education, our school has set up undergraduate teaching reform experiment class since 2003, Project, Jiangxi Province became the first three undergraduate colleges teaching reform pilot project. In the International School of Economics and Management on the basis of the school set up a special international college to further strengthen management.

The students of the cooperative education program are all enrolled in the school. In accordance with the principle of resource sharing, the Sino-British cooperative education program can share the above resources in the same way as other teaching units of the school. To ensure the quality of education, teaching and management of the Sino-British Joint Education Program, the Civil Engineering College is designated as a teaching unit, which is responsible for the teaching organization, student management, coordination and co-operation of the Sino-British cooperation in running schools.

In teaching, the college will be integrated into the college curricula of foreign universities, the introduction of foreign original textbooks, most of the professional courses taught by foreign teachers and foreign professors. At present, we have established several short-term exchange students, such as "1 + 2 + 1", "2 + 2", "3 + 1" and "3 + 2". Two school degrees. And more than 20 foreign universities, such as Troy University, Bridgeport University, Swinburne University of Technology, University of Greenwich, University of New Brunswick, Irkutsk State University of Russia, Russia Training personnel. In recent years, more than 30 students took part in various long and short-term cooperation projects with foreign universities. After graduation, most of them are engaged in accounting and financial management. Graduates are greatly welcomed by government agencies, multinational corporations, large state-owned enterprises, accounting firms and other employers.

School education and teaching highlight practical skills, innovative spirit, the Chinese culture as the foundation, the multi-cultural integration into the campus, so that students in the Chinese and foreign cultural interaction of the international community to exchange learning and become a real "international".

International College welcome you to apply for outstanding students, National Chiao Tung University will help you realize your dream of life.
